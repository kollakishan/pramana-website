• Accelerobotics
  • An innovative workshop to hone your Microcontroller skills with one of the most interesting
    transducers available, the Accelerometer.
  • Accelerometer in your phones, gaming consoles, PDAs, music players, digital cameras and
    many others!!
  • Concepts of AVR microcontrollers (Atmega8/32) and ADC and design advanced
    applications based on the Accelerometer.

It also finds the most varied industrial usage in construction, vehicle collisions, health monitoring machinery, breaking systems, navigation, image stabilization etc. You will learn to interface the Accelerometer with their Microcontrollers and get it to work in applications.
