 A quadcopter is a type of multicopter that has four rotors. . Due to the ease of construction
and precise control and hover advantage, quadcopter UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles)
are often a choice for beginners in model aircraft designs.

The objective of the workshop is to provide comprehensive hands-on training on multicopter
systems. The two days workshop involves a blend of theoryand hands-on to understand,
build from scratch and fly a quadcopter. A brain storming session is scheduled at the end
of the workshop to explore ideas about different applications of the quadcopter. Venue:
